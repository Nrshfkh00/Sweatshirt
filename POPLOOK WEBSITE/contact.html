About us


Ashura is a fashion brand since 2010. We produce attractive Ready-To-Wear for modest fashion ladies. Established from a textile background, we venture into creating our own fashion brands. We strive to offer our clothing at an affordable price made for all of ages group. In the recent 10 years, Ashura has gain popularity among modest ladies, which results in the opening of more than 10 retail stores across Malaysia. Now with online stores, we aim to bring our products closer to you, so you could shop anytime and anywhere you are.
